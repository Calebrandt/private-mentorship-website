Billing

Standard billing

Each family bought a contract with a set number of hours (e.g. 24 hr/month). When you mark a Standard session complete, the session's hours are deducted from that contract balance automatically. This is the default — most sessions are Standard.

Complimentary billing

No deduction. Use it when the session shouldn't cost the family hours:

  • Make-up — you cancelled a previous session and want to make the family whole.
  • Brief check-in / follow-up — a short conversation that isn't a real session.
  • Onboarding or trial session — a first meeting on the house.
  • Goodwill — extra time you're giving as a kindness.

Carry-over hours (coming soon)

When a contract ends with unused hours (almost every family has 2–15 left over), those hours move into a "bank balance" the family can use for longer sessions, study intensives, special outings, or extras outside the recurring schedule. Once that system is live, you'll be able to suggest the family use those banked hours instead of letting them sit.

You're a contractor — you decide which billing fits each session. Every choice is logged in the audit trail forever (sessions can never be erased or rewritten). If a family ever disputes hours, this record protects you and them.
