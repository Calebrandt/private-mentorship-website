About

A steady presence, a quiet routine.

Sarah has worked in senior care across Greater Vancouver for more than fifteen years. Her practice centers on continuity — long-term presence in the same household, daily observation of small changes, and the patience to hold steady through difficult days.

She came to Private Mentorship in 2023 from a hospital companion-care program and has served eight families since. Her work pairs companionship with the practical running of a senior's day: meals, light cleaning, appointments, and the kind of unhurried conversation that turns a long afternoon into a good one.

Sarah is most often requested by families navigating early dementia, post-surgical recovery, or the transition from independent living to in-home support. She is comfortable being a steady part of the household without taking it over.
