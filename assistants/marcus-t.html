About

A steady handoff from school to home.

Marcus has tutored math, science, and English at the middle-school level since 2019 and joined Private Mentorship in 2024. His sessions usually run school pickup through dinner — homework, an outdoor activity or scheduled lesson, and the kind of low-key conversation that helps a kid settle.

Families request him for the reliable handoff from school to home life: backpack unpacked, snack handled, homework underway by the time the second parent gets home. He works most often with children in grades 4 through 8.

Marcus drives between school, activities, and home in his own vehicle, carries a clean Class 5 BC license, and is fluent in English with conversational French.
