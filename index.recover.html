What your assistant does

One person.
Three ways to show up.

Your assistant isn't just a tutor or a helper — they're a full-spectrum support system built around your family's real life.

Role 01

Education

Your assistant works as a private teacher — covering homework, school subjects, tutoring sessions, and building lasting study habits tailored to each person's unique learning style.

Role 02

Life Skills & Mentorship

Think Big Brother / Big Sister — for all ages. Your assistant builds a real relationship, guiding confidence, social skills, community involvement, and personal growth over time.

Role 03

Daily Personal Support

A true personal assistant — booking appointments, taking notes, sending emails, coordinating with schools and organizations, and handling whatever the day brings your family.

The platform

Everything managed.
Nothing missed.

Your assistant and your family stay connected through a private app built for how real support actually works.

Task & Homework Tracking

Every assignment, to-do, and goal assigned, tracked, and reviewed inside the app. Nothing slips through.

Session Scheduling

Sessions booked, confirmed, and logged directly in the platform. Rescheduling and reminders built in.

Direct Messaging

Stay connected with your assistant anytime. Files, updates, and real-time communication in one thread.

Progress Reports

Detailed session logs, hours tracking, and milestone updates give families full visibility at every step.

Documents & Portfolio

Every resource, report, and material organized in a private Google Drive folder for your family.

Family Profiles

Multiple people in one account. Each family member gets personalized care with their own dedicated assistant.
