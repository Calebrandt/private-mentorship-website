Overview

Custom Plans are for families whose needs go beyond standard 24- or 40-hour monthly hours. Care is provided in your home by a long-term Private Family Assistant, on a written contract of three, six, or twelve months. Daytime, overnight, travel, and short-term live-in are all available.

Standard plans ($30–$45 / hour, month-to-month) cover most families. See standard plans →

Pricing & commitment

Active hours: $36 – $45 per hour. Final rate depends on tier and term length.

Live-In presence: $20 – $25 per hour. Most live-in hours are on-site presence, not active work.

Overnight awake: Premium rate, set on intake.

Travel costs: Pass-through and pre-approved.

Term discounts

3-month term: Standard rate.

6-month term: 5–8% per-hour discount.

12-month term: 10–12% per-hour discount.

No surprises. Every line item is written into the contract before service begins.

Common questions

Why isn't there fixed pricing per program?

Hours, structure, and rate vary too much by family. The tier figures above are anchors; the final plan is sized on a 30-minute intake call.

Will the same Assistant be with us long-term?

Yes. One Assistant per family for the contract term. For Live-In we rotate two or three Assistants, but the team stays the same.

Can the plan flex during the term?

Hours can move up or down with thirty days' notice. A bigger structural change triggers a fresh contract.

Do you work with funding sources or private pay only?

Both. Most clients pay privately; we also work with ICBC, CLBC, and AFU funding when applicable. Discussed on intake.

What about background checks and vetting?

Every Assistant clears a criminal record check, references, and an in-person vetting interview before joining a family. Same standard for every contract.

Where should I read the program details?

The four category pages above. Each one covers what the Assistant helps with, how the service runs, and the questions that program raises most often.
