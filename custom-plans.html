Add-ons

Layer on top of any plan.

Travel, overnight, and vacation coverage can be added to any anchor tier or quoted on their own.

Travel

Your Assistant accompanies the family on vacation or relocation. Per-diem covers travel, lodging, meals. Hourly rate continues during active hours, with reduced "on-call" rate for downtime.

Overnight

Overnight presence on top of a daytime plan. Awake-overnight is billed at a premium rate; sleep-overnight is billed at a reduced presence rate. Clear in writing before we start.

Vacation Coverage

Short-term intensive support during a defined window — school breaks, parent business trips, recovery periods. Two-week minimum, billed up front, fully refundable if cancelled 14 days out.

Common questions

A few things families always ask.

Why isn't this on the main pricing page?

Custom plans are quote-based, not menu-priced. Hour counts, structure, and rate vary by family. Putting a fixed price on every shape would mislead more often than it would help — so we anchor with starting figures and finalise on a call.

Can my plan change month to month?

Within the contract length you signed, hours and structure can flex up or down with 30 days' notice. Bigger structural changes (going from Daytime to Live-In, for example) trigger a fresh contract.

How does pricing work for Live-In?

Live-In is billed at a presence rate (lower per-hour than active hourly) because most hours are "available" rather than active work. The total monthly figure reflects the rotation of 2–3 Assistants, room and board if applicable, and the longer commitment.

Do you support travel and relocation?

Yes — Assistants travel with families when continuity matters. Travel days are billed actively; downtime on the trip (e.g. when the family is independent) is reduced presence rate. Travel costs (flights, hotel, meals, mileage) are pass-through.

Is there a minimum commitment?

3 months for Intensive Day and Full Daytime. 6 months for Extended Care. Live-In and Bridge start at 1 month for short transitions, with custom contracts for longer engagements. Longer commitments earn a discount.

What if our needs don't match any of the four tiers?

That's the normal case. The four tiers are anchors so you have a sense of the shape and cost. Most families end up with something in between — say a 6-hour weekday block plus weekends, or Daytime plus selected overnights. Tell us what you need on the form below and we'll build it.
