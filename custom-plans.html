Overview

Custom Plans are for families whose needs go beyond standard 24- or 40-hour monthly hours — daily coverage, overnights, travel companionship, or live-in support through a transitional season. Quote-based and contracted at 3, 6 or 12 month terms.

Standard plans ($30–$45 / hour, month-to-month) cover most families. See standard plans →

Tier I — Intensive Day

Shape: Daily 4-hour blocks across the school week. Same Assistant, same time each day.

Best for: Routine and continuity. Families who want a steady weekday presence without full-day coverage.

Tier II — Full Daytime

Shape: Full weekday coverage, typically 8 AM – 4 PM (flexible).

Best for: Working parents. School pick-ups, appointments, errands, dinner help — handled.

Note: Most-requested tier. 6 / 12-month terms earn a per-hour discount.

Tier III — Extended Care

Shape: Wake-up through bedtime weekday coverage via a two-Assistant rotation.

Best for: Families needing near-continuous weekday presence. Overnight add-on available.

Tier IV — Bridge / Live-In

Shape: 24/7 presence via a rotation of 2–3 Assistants, weekends and overnights included.

Best for: Transitional seasons — post-hospital recovery, post-loss, parent travel, school break, longer recovery windows.

Note: Held to a separate contract template and a vetted Live-In Assistant pool. Engagements longer than 3 months use a custom contract.

Coverage & add-ons

Layer onto any tier or contracted on their own

Travel

Use: Assistant accompanies the family on vacation or relocation for continuity.

Billing: Active hours at standard rate. Downtime at reduced presence rate. Travel costs (flights, hotel, meals, mileage) pass-through and pre-approved.

Overnight

Use: Overnight presence layered on a daytime plan, or as part of Live-In.

Billing: Awake-overnight at premium rate (active through the night). Sleep-overnight at reduced presence rate (on-site and available, but resting). Documented in writing before service begins.

Vacation coverage

Use: Short-term intensive support during a defined window — school breaks, parent business trips, recovery periods.

Billing: Two-week minimum, billed up front, refundable if cancelled 14+ days out.

Pricing & commitment

Custom plans are quote-based rather than menu-priced — the four tiers above list starting figures, and the final plan reflects the actual hours, days, and structure your family needs.

Rate types

Standard active: $36 – $45 / hour. Higher for shorter intensives, lower for longer commitments.

Live-In presence: $20 – $25 / hour. Most live-in hours are on-site presence rather than active work.

Overnight awake: Premium rate, set on intake.

Overnight sleep: Reduced presence rate.

Travel downtime: Reduced presence rate. Travel costs pass-through.

Term discounts

3-month term: Standard rate.

6-month term: 5–8% per-hour discount.

12-month term: 10–12% per-hour discount.

No-surprise pricing. Every line item — hourly rate, mileage, travel, overnight, holidays — is written into the contract before service begins.

What's included

Process

Three steps, typically two to three weeks from inquiry to first day

  1. Inquiry & call. Submit the form below or email us. We respond within one business day and schedule a 30-minute intake call to understand your situation.
  2. Written proposal. Within a few business days you receive a written plan covering hours, structure, Assistant profile, monthly cost, contract length, and start date.
  3. Match & begin. On acceptance we match the Assistant (or rotation), schedule an in-home orientation, and begin service on the contract date.

Common questions

Why isn't this on the main pricing page?

Custom plans are quote-based, not menu-priced. Hour counts, structure, and rate vary too much by family to publish fixed prices. The four tier figures above are anchors — the final plan is sized on a call.

Can the plan change month-to-month?

Within your term, hours flex up or down with 30 days' notice. A bigger structural change (Daytime to Live-In, for example) triggers a fresh contract.

How does Live-In pricing actually work?

Billed at a presence rate (~$20–25 / hour) because most live-in hours are on-site availability, not active work. The monthly figure reflects the rotation of 2–3 Assistants and any room-and-board arrangement. Awake-overnight active hours are billed at a separate premium rate.

Do you support travel and relocation?

Yes. Active hours at standard rate, downtime at reduced presence rate, travel costs pass-through and pre-approved.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Three months for Intensive Day and Full Daytime, six months for Extended Care, one month for short-transition Bridge / Live-In. Custom contract for Live-In engagements longer than three months.

What if our needs don't match any tier?

Most families land between the four tiers — say a 6-hour weekday block plus weekends, or Daytime plus selected overnights. Describe what you need on the form below and we'll build it.
