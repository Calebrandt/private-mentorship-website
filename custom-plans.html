About Custom Plans

Our standard plans of 24 or 40 reserved hours per month are built for families who want two or three sessions a week paced over the month. Custom Plans exist for families whose needs go further — daily coverage, overnight presence, travel companionship, or full-time live-in support through a transitional season.

Every custom plan is built one-to-one. The four service tiers below are anchor points, not boxes — hours, structure, and rate are finalised on a conversation. Plans run on 3-, 6-, or 12-month commitments, with discounts at the longer terms.

Standard plans are menu-priced at $30–$45 / hour, available month-to-month. Custom plans are quote-based and require a brief intake call. See standard plans.

Tier IV · Premium

Bridge / Live-In Care

A rotation of two or three Assistants providing twenty-four-hour presence through a transitional season. Designed for the moments that matter most — a post-hospital recovery, a school break, parent travel, or the loss of a primary caregiver. Live-In is the most personal and the most logistically complex service we offer, and is contracted one family at a time.

  • Coverage: 24/7 presence, weekends and overnights included
  • Team: 2–3 Assistants rotating on a documented schedule
  • Pricing: From $14,000 / month at a presence rate (~$20–25 / hour). Room and board, if applicable, is built into the contract.
  • Minimum: 1 month for short transitions; custom contract for engagements longer than 3 months
  • Note: Live-In is held to a separate contract template and a vetted Assistant pool. Travel days and overnight active hours are billed at premium rates set on intake.

Add-ons

Layer onto any tier or quote on their own

Travel

Assistant accompanies the family on vacation or relocation. Per-diem covers travel, lodging, meals. Active hours billed normally; downtime at a reduced presence rate.

Overnight

Overnight presence on top of a daytime plan. Awake-overnight is billed at a premium rate; sleep-overnight is billed at a reduced presence rate. Documented before service begins.

Vacation coverage

Short-term intensive support during a defined window — school breaks, parent business trips, recovery periods. Two-week minimum, billed up front, refundable if cancelled 14 days out.

Pricing structure

Custom plans are quote-based rather than menu-priced. The four tiers above list starting figures; the final plan reflects the actual hours, days, and structure your family needs.

How hourly rates work

Standard active rate: $36 – $45 / hour. Higher for shorter intensives, lower for longer commitments.

Live-In presence rate: $20 – $25 / hour. Most live-in hours are "on-site presence" rather than active work, so the rate is lower.

Overnight awake rate: Premium — set on intake. Awake-overnight requires the Assistant to be alert through the night.

Overnight sleep rate: Reduced presence rate. Assistant is on-site and available, but resting.

Travel: Active hours at standard rate. Downtime on the trip at reduced presence rate. Travel costs (flights, hotel, meals, mileage) are pass-through and pre-approved.

Commitment discounts

  • 3-month term: Standard rate.
  • 6-month term: Roughly 5–8% per-hour discount.
  • 12-month term: Roughly 10–12% per-hour discount.

No-surprise pricing. Every line item — hourly rate, mileage, travel, overnight, holidays — is written into your contract before service begins.

What's included in every custom plan

  • Dedicated Assistant matching based on family fit, age range, and support needs
  • Documented backup coverage so no day goes uncovered
  • All standard PM services: education, life skills, personal support

Common questions

Why isn't this on the main pricing page?

Custom plans are quote-based, not menu-priced. Hour counts, structure, and rate vary by family. Posting fixed prices for every possible shape would mislead more often than it would help — so we anchor with starting figures and finalise the rest on a call.

Can the plan change month-to-month?

Within your contract term, hours and structure can flex up or down with 30 days' notice. A bigger structural change (Daytime to Live-In, for example) triggers a fresh contract.

How does Live-In pricing actually work?

Live-In is billed at a presence rate (around $20–25 / hour) because most hours are "on-site available" rather than active work. The monthly figure reflects the rotation of 2–3 Assistants and any room-and-board arrangement. Awake-overnight active hours are billed at a premium rate set on intake.

Do you support travel and relocation?

Yes. Assistants travel with families when continuity matters. Travel time is billed actively; downtime on the trip is at the reduced presence rate. Travel costs (flights, hotel, meals, mileage) are pass-through and pre-approved.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Three months for Intensive Day and Full Daytime. Six months for Extended Care. One month for short-transition Bridge / Live-In, with custom contracts for engagements longer than three months. Longer terms earn a discount.

What if our needs don't match any of the four tiers?

That's the most common case. The four tiers are anchors so you have a sense of the shape and cost. Most families land somewhere in between — say a 6-hour weekday block plus selected weekends, or Daytime plus a few overnight nights per month.
