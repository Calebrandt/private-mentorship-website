Overview

Custom Plans are for families whose needs go beyond standard 24- or 40-hour monthly hours. Care is delivered in your home by a long-term Private Family Assistant on a written contract of three, six, or twelve months. Daytime, overnight, travel, and short-term live-in are all on the table.

Standard plans ($30 or $45 / hour, month-to-month) cover most families. See standard plans →

Why Private Mentorship

Below market on rate

Personal-care rates in Greater Vancouver typically run $37 to $55 per active hour with a three-hour minimum and a light-housekeeping clause. Custom Plans run $30 per active hour with contract-based scheduling instead of three-hour minimums.

Beyond light housekeeping

Most home-care contracts cap at light housekeeping — no heavy furniture moves, no window washing, no carpet cleaning, no scrubbing floors, no outdoor work. We don't apply that ceiling. Your Assistant handles light housekeeping daily and takes on the occasional heavy-duty task — a deep clean, a furniture move, a one-off outdoor project — typically once or twice a month, agreed in advance with the family.

One Assistant, long-term

Most agencies rotate caregivers based on weekly availability. Our Assistants are matched once, at intake, and stay with your family for the full contract term. For Live-In we rotate a fixed team of two or three; the team doesn't change.

Direct contact

You communicate with the actual team member who signed your contract. No outsourced intake centre, no shift-handoff confusion, no rotating account manager. The relationship is the product.

Common questions

Why $30 / hour for Custom Plans?

Custom Plan families use hours at the same intensity as our 1-month standard plan, so they get the same per-hour rate. Standard 2-month plans are $45 / hour because hours are spread over twice the time, which costs us more in scheduling overhead — that math doesn't apply at Custom Plan volumes.

Will the same Assistant be with us long-term?

Yes. One Assistant per family for the contract term. For Live-In we rotate two or three Assistants, but the team stays the same across the contract.

Can the plan flex during the term?

Hours move up or down with thirty days' notice. A bigger structural change — say, Daytime to Live-In — triggers a fresh contract.

Do you work with funding sources?

Yes. Most clients pay privately; we also work with ICBC, CLBC, and AFU funding when applicable. Covered on the intake call.

What about background checks and vetting?

Every Assistant clears a criminal record check, references, and an in-person vetting interview before joining a family. Same standard on every contract.

Where do I read the program details?

The four category pages above. Each one covers what the Assistant helps with, how the service runs, and the questions that program raises most often.
