    Who this fits

    Private Mentorship is for families with at least one of the following needs.

    • A family member entering or attending a learning, vocational, ESL, or post-secondary program.
    • Institutional meetings (IEPs, CLBC, WorkBC, medical, school) that need someone present and informed.
    • Funding applications, paperwork, or programs sitting unfinished for weeks or months.
    • A loved one who would benefit from a familiar adult building life skills and routines week over week.
    • A household juggling tutors, drivers, coordinators, and case managers that don't talk to each other.
    • Someone you know who needs ongoing support — gifted hours, sponsored contracts, or trusted referrals welcome.
