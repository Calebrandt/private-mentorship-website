Step 14 of 24 · Background check

Trust & safety verification

Because you will work with families and minors, we require a specific level of screening.

Important

You must request a Vulnerable Sector (PIC-VS) check. Standard criminal record checks will be rejected.

Where to apply

Choose the agency that serves your area.

RCMP — Criminal Record ChecksPIC vs PIC-VS — we require PIC-VS Vancouver Police (VPD)Standard Plus Vulnerable Sector Screening BC GovernmentCriminal Record Check (Applicants)

Fee

Paid by applicant

Time limit

Issued within 3 months

What PIC-VS includes: criminal convictions, outstanding charges, and other judicial orders. May include certain non-convictions where you were a bona fide suspect in a founded substantiated offence. Does not include unsubstantiated allegations.

How to apply: visit your local RCMP detachment or police agency and bring two pieces of government-issued ID (one with a photo). You are responsible for any fees, which vary by agency.
